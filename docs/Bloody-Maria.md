## Ingredients & Proportion

- 60 ml Tequila
- 180 ml tomato juice
- 5 ml Horseradish
- 3 dashes Hot sauce
- 3 dashes Worcestershire Sauce
- 1 dash lime juice
- 3 dashes celery salt
- 3 dashes papper

## Garnish

- Celery
- Lime

## How to make

- Combine ingredients over ice and stir
- Top with the tomato juice and celery salt
- Add celery stalk and lime for garnish and serve
